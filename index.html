Skills

After almost 10 years experience as an Assitant Editor, I have become very proficient in two of the most powerful pieces of software in post production - Avid Media Composer and DaVinci Resolve.

Avid Media Composer is the de facto NLE (non-linear editor) around the world and is used extensively throughout Hollywood. It is the only editing software able to support large teams working on projects together, and I have extensive experience in setting up those environments for large teams of editors and post producers.

DaVinci Resolve has taken the over the Colour Grading space within video and is the industry standard for 'finishing' projects. Developed in Melbourne by BlackMagic Design, you would be hard pressed to find any television series or film being released without having gone through Resolve first.
